About

After four years as a customer service representative in BGC, I realized that long commutes and demanding schedules were taking a toll on my well-being and time with my kids. Determined to find a better balance, I discovered the world of virtual assistance—where I can leverage my communication, organization, and problem-solving skills to help businesses thrive remotely.

Now, I am ready to bring my dedication and expertise to clients who need reliable support.
